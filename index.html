Why Quick Insurance?

Insurance is boring. Going through the process of finding the right policy and buying it online is a nightmare for most. This was a shared sentiment with everyone we came across. We are a bunch of 20-30 year olds, who thought of this as an opportunity to bring about a change. When the whole world is moving towards giving consumers a wonderful online experience of buying food, clothes, houses and everything under the sun; then why not buying insurance be that easy too? So we have tried to give you a place where comparing, researching and buying an insurance is painless and easy. We don't spam. Our recommendations are solely based on the data you provide about yourself, and not based on any affiliations.
